# James Blond Rentals — Backlink outreach plan

_Goal: build 15–25 high-quality referring domains over the next 90 days to lift rankings for
"car hire christchurch", "truck hire wellington", "van hire auckland" and long-tail location
queries. Focus on NZ-relevant, topically aligned sites — cheap link farms are ignored._

Auckland Airport, West Auckland, South Auckland, Wellington, Christchurch, Christchurch
Central, Hamilton) with a consistent NAP (name, address, phone) — Google uses this
for local pack ranking.

## Priority 5 — Community & sponsorships (long term)

Small NZ-relevant sponsorships that reliably earn do-follow links from real
community sites:

- Sponsor a Christchurch touch-rugby or netball club → link from club site
- Sponsor a local moving-house charity (Habitat for Humanity ReStore vehicle)
- University clubs (UC, Vic, AUT) needing vans for events — offer partner rate
- Local Facebook Marketplace / Neighbourly groups — brand awareness, not links
- Community events sponsorship (Round the Bays, Coast to Coast)

## Anchor-text strategy

Avoid over-optimised, keyword-stuffed anchors ("cheap car hire Christchurch cheap
rental Christchurch"). Aim for a natural mix:

- 40% branded ("James Blond Rentals", "James Blond")
- 30% naked URL / "click here" / "website"
- 20% partial-match ("car hire in Christchurch", "Wellington van rentals")
- 10% exact-match ("car hire Christchurch") — used sparingly on the best links

## Tracking

- Add referring domains to the SEO tracker weekly.
- Log outreach in a shared sheet: contact, date, status, link acquired.
- Re-run Semrush backlink audit at day 30 and day 90.
